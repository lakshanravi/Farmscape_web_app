Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy.
Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our FARMSCAPE Web Application.

Collection of Data

We may collect and process the following types of information:

Personal Identification Information: Farmer details, such as name, email address, phone number, and any other data voluntarily submitted during registration or form submissions.

Animal Information: Details related to your animals, including health records, vaccination history, feeding schedules, and identification tags.

Non-Personal Identification Information: Technical details such as browser type, operating system, IP address, and usage data related to the web application.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Cookies and similar technologies may be used to enhance your user experience and gather information about how you interact with the application.

Usage of Data

How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect in the following ways:

- To help you manage farming operations effectively, such as organizing animal health records and vaccination schedules.
- To personalize the application and respond better to your specific farming needs.
- To improve the functionality of the application based on feedback and usage data.
- To notify you about updates, features, or important changes to the application.
- To ensure the security of your data and prevent unauthorized access.

How We Protect Your Information

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information, animal health records, transaction data, and other information stored in the application.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We may share anonymized or aggregated data with trusted partners to improve our services or for research purposes. Personal data may be shared only when legally required or with your explicit consent.

Your Consent

By using our application, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the collection, use, and sharing of your data as described herein.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to periodically review this page for any changes. Your continued use of the application after updates to the policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications.
